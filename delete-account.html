Account & Data Deletion Policy – Laundry Lens

Last Updated: December 2025

Laundry Lens (“the App”, “we”, “us”, or “our”), developed and published by Ramzeen BARBOZA, is committed to protecting user privacy and providing a transparent process for account deletion and data removal requests.

1. User Right to Account Deletion

Users may request the permanent deletion of their Laundry Lens account at any time. Approved requests will result in the deletion of all personally identifiable information, except where temporary retention is required for legal or compliance purposes.

2. How to Submit a Deletion Request

To delete your account:

  • Send an email to: support@laundrylens.com
  • Use the subject: “Account Deletion Request – Laundry Lens”
  • Include in the email body:
    • The email address linked to your Laundry Lens account
    • Optional: User ID (if known)
    • Optional: Reason for deletion (not required)

For security reasons, deletion requests cannot be submitted via social networks, phone calls, or third-party services.

4. Data That May Be Retained Temporarily

Data TypeStatusRetention Period

Retained data is encrypted, non-identifiable, and cannot be used to recover or reactivate a deleted account.

5. Deletion Processing Timeline

  • Requests are processed within 7 business days after validation.
  • Users receive a confirmation email before final deletion.
  • If additional verification is required, we may request a secure re-authentication via Firebase.

6. Effects of Account Deletion

Once deleted:

  • Access to the App and its account-based features is revoked.
  • Deletion is permanent and irreversible.
  • No backup remains that can restore personal data.
  • A new account can only be created by registering again manually.

7. Security & Privacy Commitments

Laundry Lens ensures:

  • Encrypted data transfers via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Secure storage under Google Firebase infrastructure.
  • No sale or external sharing of personal user data.
  • Strict compliance with international data protection principles (GDPR-aligned).

8. Developer Legal Identity

9. Abuse & False Declarations

Submitting fraudulent or manipulated information may result in the inability to process deletion requests and could lead to removal of access to Laundry Lens services for security and regulatory protection.

10. Additional Notes

Laundry Lens relies on Firebase services including Authentication, Firestore, and Cloud Messaging. Account deletion includes removal of identifiers linked to these services, while non-identifiable anonymized statistics may be retained for a limited period for internal performance improvement.
